## Overview

This task involved researching and documenting the critical role of patch management in cybersecurity. The report covers why patches matter, the vulnerability lifecycle, real-world breaches caused by unpatched systems (WannaCry and Equifax), the consequences of not patching, the patch management lifecycle, best practices, and common challenges.

The goal was to demonstrate the ability to research complex security topics, synthesise information from credible sources, and present findings in a structured, professional format suitable for a technical or management audience.

## Report Structure

The report is structured as follows:

1. **Introduction** – Definition of patch management and its role in the vulnerability lifecycle.
2. **Why Patches Matter** – The vulnerability lifecycle, CVE assignment, CVSS scoring, and the exploitation window.
3. **WannaCry Ransomware (2017)** – How a two-month-old patch could have prevented 300,000+ infections.
4. **Equifax Data Breach (2017)** – How an unpatched Apache Struts vulnerability led to a $700 million settlement.
5. **Consequences of Not Patching** – Data breaches, ransomware, compliance violations, financial penalties, operational disruption, reputational damage.
6. **The Patch Management Lifecycle** – 5 phases: Discovery, Assessment, Testing, Deployment, Verification.
7. **Patch Management Best Practices** – 7-step checklist for organisations.
8. **Challenges and How to Overcome Them** – Legacy systems, downtime concerns, testing requirements, lack of visibility, no executive buy-in, patch volatility.
9. **Conclusion** – 3 key takeaways for organisations.
10. **References** – 8 credible sources.

## Methodology

The research was conducted using:

- **NIST** (National Institute of Standards and Technology) – NIST SP 800-40 Rev. 4 (Enterprise Patch Management Planning).
- **CISA** (Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency) – Threat intelligence and vulnerability alerts.
- **MITRE Corporation** – CVE database and CVSS scoring framework.
- **Security news outlets** (Qualys, ISACA, U.S. Government Equifax Settlement) – Real-world case studies.
